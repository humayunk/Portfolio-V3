Helping educators grade tests on-the-go.

Overview

In 2016, Akindi, a popular web platform that helps teachers with scantron-based multiple choice tests commissioned Tiny Hearts to help them take their platform on mobile. The goal was to make it dead simple for educators to create assessments, scan them, and get real-time feedback on how the students performed, all on their trusty iPhone.
My Role: I was the lead product designer and product owner working closely with the client to help identify the project requirements and create an experience that would achieve their goals. Once we had the UX figured out, I worked with the talented Dmitri Litvinov to execute on the visual design.

Assessment Feed

Creating an assessment is a easy as naming it and picking a template. Educators can then either air print or email it to themselves to print later.

Hassle-free Scanning

With touch-free scanning, swipping up to delete, and taping a scan to edit, we focused on making this part of the process as pain-free as possible.

Real-Time Results

As soon as a educator scans the assessments they can instantiously view results and pick out the average score and outliers amongst students.

Results

The Akindi iOS app is helping the start-up gain additional traction in the competitive edtech landscape and has helped locked down institutional clients that will help spur growth and continued innovation in the space.
