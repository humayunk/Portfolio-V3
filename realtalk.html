A career app that gets real with students.

Overview

In 2015, The Learning Partnership commissioned Tiny Hearts to help them tackle the challenge of helping high school students make smarter post-secondary decisions.
My Role: I was the lead product designer and product owner working closely with the client, their board, and a braintrust consisting of educators and students to help define strategy and design.

The Feed

Recognizing that stories are the best way to communicate insights, advice, and personal learnings, the app enables students to scroll through and disover hundreds of career profiles.

Visual Filters

We knew that some students would identify with certain careers over others so we wanted to make it easy for them to search and discover the most relevant profiles with no more than two taps.

Bookmarks and Resources

We created this section help students bookmark profiles and save resources as a checklist to explore later.

Results

Real Talk is currently a 5-star app with more then 50,000 downloads.
The iOS app was featured as a "Best New App" upon launch and featured twice on the "Best of Education Category" on Apple's appstore.
